Biography

Blacklite District rose to international YouTube fame in 2018 with the release of his song "Cold As Ice", which ended up getting 100 million streams and hit #35 on Billboard's Mainstream Rock chart. He followed it up with a string of other viral hits, including "The Struggle", "Just So You Know", and "Goodbye" feat. ROMAN. Combined, he has gotten over 500 million streams globally across YouTube and Spotify, and was awarded YouTube's Sliver Creator Award in November 2019 for passing 100,000 subscribers on his channel.
